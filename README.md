## Technologies Used
- Frontend:React.js
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js
- Authentication: firebase
- Database: mongodb
- Styling: Tailwind CSS
- Deployment: firebase (Frontend)


---

### Description:
FinEase is an app to manage your transactions. Where you can track your transactions.It is solving your daily life Finance.Track your transactions and make your dream true is the tag line of our app.

---

## Features our website:
- Manage your transactions 
- See transactions summery
- See your transactions
- Modify your transactions
- Over view of your transactions

---

### Dependances:
To initialze the app you have to added firebase auth in an .env file.
